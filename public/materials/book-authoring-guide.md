# REJU Book Authoring Guide

**You Are Authoring Your Personalized REJU Transformation Book™**

REJU is editorial. Your daily reflections, photos, scores, milestones, and Today's Victory entries are the chapters you write. This guide explains how to build an honest, visually compelling book across 20 cohort days — or the full **42-day** journey if you want a richer volume.

## Daily Transformation Log — Field Guide

### Identity & Program Day

- **Participant ID** — from registration (required)
- **Full Name** — as you want it printed in your book
- **Telegram** — optional, for cohort support
- **Day of Program** — 1 through 42 (20-day cohort + optional extension)
- **Date** — calendar date of the entry

### Physical & Skin Observations

- **Physical Condition** — honest narrative: energy, digestion, sleep, pain, mobility
- **Skin Description** — texture, tone, clarity, areas you are tracking
- **Skin Clarity / Dryness / Inflammation** — rate 1–5; trends matter more than single scores

### Mind & Compliance

- **Energy Level / Mental Clarity / Mood** — rate 1–5 daily
- **Compliance** — Yes or No to the REJU Protocol that day
- **Non-Compliance Reason** — if No, write why (this is data, not failure)

### Photos

Upload daily photos when possible:

- Same angles and lighting when you can
- Front, side, and any focus areas
- Photos anchor visual transformation — memory forgets; your book will not

### Today's Victory (11 Positive Things + Reflections)

- List positive observations — however small
- **What I Notice Today** — body's language, cravings, breakthroughs
- **Changes Since Yesterday** — compare honestly to prior entry
- End with **one Today's Victory** — the single best thing about today

### Consent

Confirm consent to include your materials in your compiled Transformation Book.

## Writing Tips for an Amazing Book

**Write in your own voice.** Authenticity reads better than polish. This is your legacy, not a performance.

**Be consistent.** One entry per program day, even when motivation is low — especially then.

**Document the rollercoaster.** Frustration, doubt, clarity, and joy are all valid chapters. Transformation is not linear.

**Avoid scale obsession.** Focus on lived experience, photos, energy, skin, and compliance — not a number on a scale.

**Use the 20-day cohort sessions** as your live anchor; continue through **Day 42** if you want a longer, deeper book.

**At Day 42**, contact REJU personnel to generate your final compiled Personalized REJU Transformation Book.
